About Us

At Slumbr, we aim to take the stress out of having people over for the weekend by making sure the people close to you always have a place to stay. Our custom Slumbr air mattresses are designed to be as durable as they are comfortable. Never again do you have to worry about your guests complaining about how your lumpy matress kept them up all night, and gone are the days of having lie about having washed the sheets they're wrapping around their body.

We give our mattresses, sheets, and pillows a thorough cleaning after every use so you dont have to! In addition, our staff will check for any damage to the air mattresses before sending them out to ensure you never get a faulty bed.

We are dedicated to providing a quality sleep experience for your guests. All orders are backed by a money back guarantee. If any part of your experience isn't up to par please contact us at support@slum.br and a customer representative will work to get your issue resolved as soon as possible.
